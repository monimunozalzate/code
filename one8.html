FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Discover the answers to common questions regarding our services and the owner-operator trucking industry.

What do I need to partner with One8 Freight?

To partner with One8 Freight, please give us a call. Our team will be happy to guide you through the process and answer any questions you may have.

We work with different trailer types and are actively hiring CDL drivers for Dry van and Reefer or refrigerated loads.

What are the benefits of working with One8 Freight?

As a partner with One8 Freight, you can benefit from choosing your routes, which allows for better time management, less waiting time for loads, and the ability to customize routes based on your geographical preferences. We value and respect our truck drivers, and we are committed to helping you grow your business.

How long will the hiring process take?

The hiring process for truck driving jobs at One8 Freight typically takes a few days. We understand the importance of getting you on the road and will work diligently to get you started as soon as possible.

How often do I get loads assigned?

After you deliver your assignment, we will assign a new load as soon as possible. Our dispatch team works hard to ensure that our CDL drivers have consistent truck loads and are never without a job for an extended period of time.

How can I customize my routes?

At One8 Freight, we understand that customization is key. Each owner operator can customize their routes based on geographical preferences, allowing for flexibility and optimal efficiency.
